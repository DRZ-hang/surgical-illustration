Laparoscopic cholecystectomy · Critical View of Safety
1

overview

Expose the hepatocystic triangle and confirm only the cystic duct and cystic artery enter the gallbladder (CVS) before dividing anything; the common bile duct is never clipped or cut. Source: SAGES safe cholecystectomy guideline.
